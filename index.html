Start working on the vacancy

After the Kick-off meeting, we start working on the vacancy. Our recruitment strategy involves selecting specialized recruiters for each client project, ensuring they align with the specific requirements. This approach allows us to conduct a rigorous selection process, resulting in high-quality candidates.

Team researches

In the early stages of vacancy work, our team researches through databases, networks, and recommendations, enabling us to present initial candidates within 2-3 days. 

We adjust requirements based on feedback and gather ample information during the kick-off call. With our meticulous approach and analysis, we typically provide up to 5 candidates for successful placement.

A thorough candidate selection

We have a thorough candidate selection process using agreed-upon questions with the client. Our recruiters compile detailed reports, saving you time by assessing candidates before the first client call. Our reports include contact information, salary expectations, notice period, and approved technical and non-technical questions.

We can also perform English language checks and provide code samples if required.
